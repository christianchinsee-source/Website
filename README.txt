Tokyo Trip Website

Files included:
- index.html
- places.html
- gallery.html
- videos.html
- itinerary.html
- style.css
- script.js

How to use:
1. Open index.html in your browser.
2. Put your photos inside an images folder.
3. Put your videos inside a videos folder.
4. Replace the placeholder text with your own image or video code.

Example image:
<img src="images/shibuya.jpg" alt="Shibuya crossing in Tokyo">

Example video:
<video controls>
    <source src="videos/tokyo-trip.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

Example YouTube/Vimeo:
Paste the iframe code into the video placeholder section.
